Call for Writers

Youth Impact E‑Magazine (Cameroon)

Tell stories. Inspire change. Amplify youth voices.

Here’s your chance to join a bold new platform that celebrates, amplifies, and connects the achievements of young people in Cameroon! This e‑magazine is created by the Association of Adolescent Girls and Young Women and designed to be the most read digital magazine by young people in Cameroon.

Apply Now → Deadline: Aug 25th, 2025

About the Magazine

The Youth Impact E‑Magazine is a digital platform created for young people in Cameroon. It profiles and celebrates youth achievements across technology, agriculture, gender, climate change, music, film, politics, sports, entrepreneurship, and more. We want this to be a space where youth see themselves, share their stories, and inspire each other.

Who We’re Looking For

  • Passionate about writing and storytelling
  • Interested in youth issues and achievements
  • Ready to learn, grow, and contribute your voice

If that sounds like you — we’d love to read your work!

What You’ll Gain

  • Get your work published and read widely across Cameroon
  • Build writing & digital media skills with mentorship and peer support
  • Grow your network with young changemakers and professionals
  • Be part of a pioneering youth‑led movement inspiring the next generation

How to Apply

  1. A short motivation (max 200 words) on why you want to be a writer for the Youth Impact E‑Magazine.
  2. A short writing sample (article, essay, blog post, or creative piece — max 500 words).

Submit via the application form: https://formspree.io/f/mrblnpzr
